## Your Business Card, Reimagined

NFC.cool lets you create a digital business card and share it with a single tap. Hold your iPhone near someone else's phone or an NFC tag, and your contact info appears instantly — no app needed on their end.

### How It Works
1. **Create your card** — Add your name, title, company, phone, email, social links, and a photo
2. **Write to an NFC tag** — Program any NFC tag with your vCard data
3. **Share with a tap** — Anyone who taps the tag gets your full contact info, ready to save

### No App Required on Their End
When someone taps your NFC business card, their phone opens your contact info in the browser. They can save it with one tap — on iPhone or Android.

### Customize Your Card
- Add your photo and company logo
- Include social media links (LinkedIn, Instagram, X, and more)
- Choose from multiple card styles
- Add a custom URL to your card page

### NFC Tag Compatibility
Works with any writable NFC tag — NTAG213, NTAG215, NTAG216, and more. NFC.cool recommends NTAG216 (888 bytes) for full vCard support.

### Your Online Card Page
Every NFC.cool business card gets a shareable web page at **mycard.nfc.cool**. Share the link anywhere — email signatures, social profiles, QR codes — and people can view and save your contact info from any device.

### Better Than Paper
- **Always up to date** — Change your info anytime; the tag always shares the latest
- **Eco-friendly** — One tag replaces thousands of paper cards
- **Memorable** — A tap is more impressive than handing over a piece of cardboard
- **Trackable** — Know when your card page is visited
