## Scan in 3D

NFC.cool's 3D scanner creates detailed 3D models from your iPhone camera — objects, rooms, and everything in between.

### Object Scanning
Walk around any object and NFC.cool builds a 3D model. Export as USDZ, OBJ, or STL for 3D printing, AR, or game development.

### Room Scanning
With LiDAR-equipped devices (iPhone 12 Pro+), scan entire rooms with accurate measurements. Perfect for:
- **Interior design** — Plan your space in 3D
- **Real estate** — Create virtual walkthroughs
- **Construction** — Measure and document spaces

### Export Formats
- **USDZ** — Apple AR and 3D previews
- **OBJ** — Universal 3D format
- **STL** — 3D printing ready

### No Extra Hardware Needed
Object scanning works on all iPhones. Room scanning uses the built-in LiDAR sensor on supported devices.
