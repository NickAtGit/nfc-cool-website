## Scan & Create QR Codes

NFC.cool includes a full QR code and barcode scanner — plus a powerful QR Code Studio for creating your own.

### Instant Scanning
Point your camera at any QR code or barcode. NFC.cool recognizes it instantly and shows you what's inside.

### QR Code Studio
Create beautiful, custom QR codes:
- **URLs** — Link to any website
- **vCards** — Share contact info
- **Wi-Fi** — Let people connect with a scan
- **Email** — Pre-filled email templates
- **Custom** — Any text or data

### Design Your QR Codes
Add your brand colors and logo. Export as PNG or SVG for print, web, or social media.

### Barcode Support
Scan all major barcode formats: EAN, UPC, Code 128, Code 39, ITF, and more.
