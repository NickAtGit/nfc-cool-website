## The Complete NFC Toolkit

NFC.cool reads, writes, and programs any NFC tag — from simple URLs to complex vCards and custom data.

### Read Any Tag
Just hold your iPhone near an NFC tag. NFC.cool instantly shows what's stored on it — URLs, contact info, Wi-Fi credentials, and more.

### Write Anything
Program NFC tags with:
- **vCards** — Share your contact info with a tap
- **URLs** — Link to your website, portfolio, or social media
- **Wi-Fi** — Let guests connect to your Wi-Fi by tapping a tag
- **Custom data** — Write raw NDEF records for advanced use cases

### Business Card Mode
Create and share your digital business card via NFC. When someone taps your tag, they get your contact info — no app needed on their end.

### Supported Tag Types
NTAG213, NTAG215, NTAG216, MIFARE Classic, MIFARE Ultralight, and more. If your iPhone can read it, NFC.cool can handle it.

### 35 Languages
NFC.cool speaks your language — literally. Available in 35 languages, making NFC accessible worldwide.
