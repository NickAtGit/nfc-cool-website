**Last updated: April 2026**

### Website

This website does not set cookies, run analytics, or track visitors. Server-side request logs may be kept by the hosting provider for up to 30 days for the purpose of detecting and mitigating abuse.

If you submit the newsletter form, your email address is sent over HTTPS to a same-origin endpoint that forwards it to our email provider (Mailjet, a French/EU company) to add you to the announcement list. Mailjet stores the address only for the purpose of sending you the emails you signed up for. You can unsubscribe with the one-click link in every email; we delete the address from Mailjet on request. No cookies are set during this process.

### Apps

NFC.cool Tools and NFC.cool Business Card process your data locally on your device. Scans, NFC writes, contacts, and notes are stored only on your device - they are not uploaded to any NFC.cool server.

If you opt in to **webhooks**, scan results are sent to the URL you configure. NFC.cool is not the recipient and does not see your scan data.

If you opt in to **business-card analytics** (Business Card app only), aggregate view/save counts for your shared cards are recorded so you can see them in the app. No personal data of viewers is collected.

### Third parties

The apps use Apple's NFC, scanning, and document recognition frameworks on iPhone, and the corresponding system frameworks on Android. These run entirely on-device.

### Sub-processors

The only external services that may process limited data are:

- **Apple Inc.** - App Store distribution, App Store reviews, App Clip hosting, iCloud sync (your iCloud account, not ours). Apple's privacy practices apply.
- **Google LLC** - Google Play distribution and store reviews. Google's privacy practices apply.
- **Mailjet SAS (France/EU)** - newsletter delivery if you subscribe. Stores your email for the purpose of sending you the updates you signed up for. One-click unsubscribe in every email.
- **Cloudflare, Inc.** - serves this website. May retain server logs for up to 30 days for abuse detection.
- **GitHub, Inc.** - hosts the source repository and CI for this website. Receives a request log only when assets are fetched from a GitHub-hosted CDN (currently: none on production).

No analytics, no ad networks, no cross-site tracking. Your data is not sold or shared with any other third party.

### Retention

- Newsletter subscriber email: until you unsubscribe or request deletion.
- Server access logs (Cloudflare): up to 30 days.
- App data on your device: until you delete the app or its data.
- Mailjet's own logs: per Mailjet's retention policy (typically months, not years).

### Your rights (GDPR)

If you're in the EU/EEA you have the right to access, correct, port, or delete the personal data we hold about you. The only personal data we hold is your email address in the newsletter system (if you've subscribed). To exercise these rights, email <hello@nfc.cool>. We respond within 30 days.
