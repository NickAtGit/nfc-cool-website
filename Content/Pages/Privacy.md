**Last updated: April 2026**

### Website

This website does not set cookies, run analytics, or track visitors. Server-side request logs may be kept by the hosting provider (GitHub Pages) for up to 30 days for the purpose of detecting and mitigating abuse.

### Apps

NFC.cool Tools and NFC.cool Business Card process your data locally on your device. Scans, NFC writes, contacts, and notes are stored only on your device — they are not uploaded to any NFC.cool server.

If you opt in to **webhooks**, scan results are sent to the URL you configure. NFC.cool is not the recipient and does not see your scan data.

If you opt in to **business-card analytics** (Business Card app only), aggregate view/save counts for your shared cards are recorded so you can see them in the app. No personal data of viewers is collected.

### Third parties

The apps use Apple's NFC, scanning, and document recognition frameworks on iPhone, and the corresponding system frameworks on Android. These run entirely on-device.
