## What information do we collect?

### Personal information you disclose to us

Information voluntarily provided during registration or service interactions may include names, phone numbers, email addresses, mailing addresses, usernames, and billing addresses.

### Sensitive information

Sensitive data — details about racial or ethnic background, health, religious views, or other categories listed by applicable data protection laws — is not processed by us intentionally.

### Information automatically collected

IP addresses, browser characteristics, and device information are automatically collected. This includes:

- **Log and usage data.** Server-collected diagnostic and performance information, including IP addresses, device details, browser type, and usage timestamps.
- **Device data.** Computer, phone, or tablet information such as identification numbers, location, browser type, hardware model, and operating system details.
- **Location data.** Device location information collected via GPS and similar technologies; you can opt out through device settings.

### Payment data

All payment data is stored by Stripe.

## How do we process your information?

Information is processed for:

- Facilitating account creation and authentication.
- Fulfilling and managing orders, payments, and exchanges.
- Protecting vital interests and preventing harm.

## What legal bases do we rely on?

Processing relies on one or more of:

- **Consent.** Your permission, withdrawable at any time.
- **Performance of a contract.** Fulfilling contractual obligations and providing the service.
- **Legal obligations.** Compliance with law enforcement or regulatory requirements.
- **Vital interests.** Protecting safety and preventing potential threats.

## When and with whom do we share your personal information?

### Third-party services

Personal information may be shared with:

- **Google Analytics** — for tracking and analyzing service usage; opt-out available at <https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout>.
- **Google Maps Platform APIs** — for mapping and location services.
- **AdMob** — for targeted advertising; collects device information and user interests.
- **Firebase** — for analytics, crash reporting, and performance metrics.
- **Stripe** — for payment processing.
- **Affiliate marketing programs** — to track referral success.

### Business transfers

Information may be shared in connection with mergers, asset sales, financing, or business acquisitions.

### Google AdMob

AdMob uses the Apple Advertising Identifier for creating pseudonymous user profiles and targeted advertising. The Apple Ad-ID is transmitted to and stored on a Google server in the USA. You can opt out by disabling tracking in Privacy settings. Processing aligns with legitimate interests under GDPR Article 6.

### Facebook tracking pixel

The Facebook Tracking Pixel collects user interactions (page views, clicks, engagement metrics) and conversion data from advertising campaigns. This data optimizes marketing and enables personalized advertising. You can adjust cookie settings or use [Facebook Ad Preferences](https://www.facebook.com/help/247395082112892) to opt out.

## Cookies and other tracking technologies

Cookies and related tracking technologies (such as pixels and web beacons) may be used to retrieve or store data. Cookie preferences can be managed through the website's cookie consent interface.

**Marketing cookies** track browsing behavior for personalized advertisements and promotional content. Detailed information and preference management are available in the cookie consent section.

## How long do we keep your information?

We will only keep your personal information for as long as it is necessary for the purposes set out in this privacy notice, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. When legitimate business needs no longer exist, information is deleted, anonymized, or securely isolated from further processing.

## How do we keep your information safe?

Organizational and technical security measures are implemented, though no online platform can provide 100% security, and you should be aware of inherent internet risks.

## Do we collect information from minors?

The service does not intentionally collect data from individuals under 18. By using the Services, you represent that you are at least 18 or that you are the parent or guardian of such a minor and consent to such minor dependent's use of the Service. If minor data is discovered, accounts are deactivated and information is promptly deleted.

## What are your privacy rights?

In regions including the EEA, UK, Switzerland, and Canada, you may have rights to:

- Request access to personal information.
- Request rectification or erasure.
- Restrict processing.
- Data portability.
- Object to automated decision-making.

### Withdrawing consent

Consent can be withdrawn at any time by contacting us, though this does not affect the lawfulness of prior processing.

### Opting out of marketing communications

You can unsubscribe from marketing via email unsubscribe links or by contacting support.

### Account information

You may review, change, or terminate your account by contacting us. Upon termination, information is deleted from active databases, though some data may be retained for fraud prevention, investigations, and legal compliance.

### Cookies and similar technologies

Most browsers accept cookies by default but allow you to reject or remove them, though this may affect certain features.

## Controls for Do-Not-Track features

No uniform technology standard currently exists for Do-Not-Track signals. We will update our practices if such standards are adopted.

## Do we make updates to this notice?

We may update this privacy notice from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Revised" date and the updated version will be effective as soon as it is accessible. Material changes may be communicated through prominent notices or direct notifications.
