## Privacy Policy

NFC.cool believes your data is yours. This app stores everything locally on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or share your personal data.

### Data We Collect
We do not collect personal data. Period.

### Data Stored on Your Device
- NFC tag contents you've scanned or written
- QR codes you've created
- Documents you've scanned
- 3D models you've created
- App preferences and settings

All of this stays on your device. We have no access to it.

### Analytics
We use anonymized, aggregated analytics to understand app usage patterns and improve the product. This data cannot be linked to you personally.

### Third-Party Services
NFC.cool uses Apple's native frameworks for subscriptions. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We never see your payment details.
