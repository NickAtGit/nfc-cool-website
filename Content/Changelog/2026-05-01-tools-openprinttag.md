NFC.cool Tools 6.11.0 ships native support for the **OpenPrintTag** standard - the cross-vendor NFC data format for 3D-printing spools.

What's new:

- **Scan any OpenPrintTag spool** and read material, length, brand, batch info in a consistent format.
- **Write OpenPrintTag fields** to blank NTAG tags - material type, brand, length, custom notes.
- **Update spool status** as you print, without needing brand-specific apps.
- **Expert Mode** lets you inspect raw NDEF records for debugging or advanced workflows.

OpenPrintTag is an open initiative by Prusa Research aiming to unify smart-spool data across the 3D-printing ecosystem. Adding native support means NFC.cool is now a practical tool for makers managing filament across multiple brands.

Available now on [iOS](https://ios.nfc.cool) and the latest [Android](https://android.nfc.cool) release. Free.
